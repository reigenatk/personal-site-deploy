Reaction Wars

Do you ever get a stupid idea and decide to just act on it? Well that was this project in a nutshell. Except it actually ended up not being so stupid, and the entire thing was actually way harder to implement than it seemed.

I was browing youtube and I watched a video about how coding is best learned by doing actual projects. And having not done a project in a while, I wanted to code. But what to code? Well I knew there were a ton of really cool node.js packages out there, and socket.io was one I had heard of.

So I naturally bought a course and learned a bit about the package. However, I had difficulties thinking of a good way to apply this package. Well, why not just make the most obvious game possible- a reaction time test! Two users face off and depending on how fast their fingers are (and also their ping from the server lol) they either win or lose!
